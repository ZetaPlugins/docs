ZetaPlugins

Welcome to ZetaPlugins where we create high-quality, open-source plugins for Minecraft. Our plugins are highly customizable but also easy to use.

Plugin Docs​

You can find the docs for our plugins in the sidebar. If you want to go back to this page, just click on the logo in the top left corner.

Contributing​

We always welcome feedback, suggestions, and contributions from the community.

To contribute, please submit a pull request to the relevant repository. Each project may have its own contributing guidelines, so be sure to check those first.

You can also share your feedback or ideas in our official Discord server.

If you're interested in helping translate a plugin, please refer to that plugin's documentation. Most of our plugins use GitLocalize for translations, but you're also welcome to submit a pull request with the appropriate translation files.

Support​

If you need help with one of our plugins, please first check the respective documentation. If you can't find the answer there, feel free to reach out to us on our Discord server.

Our Plugins​

  • LifeStealZ - LifeStealZ is a lifesteal SMP plugin, that allows you to steal hearts from other players, when you kill them. If a player has no more hearts, he is eliminated.
  • ServerLinksZ - A Minecraft plugin, that allows you to add Links to the "Server Links" page in the pause menu. ServerLinksZ also adds a command for users to quickly look up links like /discord.
  • CookieClickerZ - A Minecraft plugin that adds a Cookie Clicker game to your server. Players can click a cookie block to earn cookies and unlock upgrades, adding a fun and addictive mini-game to your server.
